Peanut Butter & Berries Parfait

This nourishing recipe is perfect for all people of all ages! It is quick and easy to make, but packed with flavour and is sure to impress your guests.

Dessert

15 min

PREP TIME

10 MIN

COOK TIME

5 MIN

TOTAL TIME

15 MIN

SERVINGS
6 persons
CUISINE
Italian
STORAGE
In fridge up to 1 month

Instructions

  1. Sugar - 1 tsp
  2. Water - 1/2 cup
  3. Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  4. Peanut Butter - 3 tsp
  5. Berries - 1 cup
